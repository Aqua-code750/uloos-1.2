Engineered For Ultra-Precision

No web wrapper. No emulation lag. Built from raw assembly and Rust to execute directly on real CPU registers.

200Hz Polling Mouse

Upgraded with hardware-level sample command multipliers (8 counts/mm precision) for perfect hand-to-cursor scaling and responsiveness.

Double-Buffered VGA

Features zero lock-contention, writing frames to a fast RAM backbuffer before swapping instantly to VGA memory at 0xA0000, eliminating screen tearing.

Retro Entertainment

Includes nostalgic classic utilities out of the box, featuring a direct playable MS-DOS prompt, UloText editor, UloSlides, and a custom TUI Doom clone.

Instant Motherboard Boot

Hardware delays calibrated from 8 million down to 100,000 cycles to initialize auxiliary devices instantly. Boots from BIOS in under 200ms.
